How you use the web

First, you should check out the internet (the world's largest computer network) connection. Then, you could communicate with your web browser. For example Chrome, Internet Explorer, Firefox, etc. On the browser, you input a URL we want like Udacity.com on the address bar. Your computer sends a HTTP Request to a server. The server shows you the right HTML web page you're looking for on your browser. This video dose a good job of explaining.

HTML

HTML means HyperText Markup Language. HTML is made up of text contents what you see, markup what it looks like, references to other documents (e.g. images, videos, etc.) and links to other pasges. This video gives you a good overview.

HTML Markup

You use a lot of tags for making your webpages. These are very useful for construction and decoration of your webpages (e.g. links to other pages, show images, etc.) The tag consist of opening tag, contents, and closing tag. You can call the whole thing 'Element'. This video gives your more specific information of HTML Markup.
